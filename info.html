Printers :-

  • ➢ Printer is an output device. which is Used to print Information on Paper.
  • ❖ There are two Types of Printers.
  • 1).Impact Printers
  • 2).Non Impact Printers

1).Impact Printers :-

  • Impact printers Print the Characters By Striking Them on the Ribbon, Which is then Pressed on the Paper.
  • ❖ Characteristics Of Impact Printers :-
  • ➢ Very Noisy
  • ➢ Useful For bulk Printing due to low cost.
  • ➢ There is Physical Contact with the Paper to Produce an image
  • ❖ There Printers are two types :-
  • 1). Character Printers.
  • 2). Line Printers.

1). Character Printers :-

  • character Printers are the Printers Which Print one character at a time.
  • ❖ These are Further Divided into two Types :-
  • 1). Dot Matrix Printer (DMP)
  • 2). Daisy Wheel

2). Line Printers :-

  • Line Printers are the Printers Which Print One Line at a time.
  • ❖ There are Of Two Types.
  • 1). Drum Printer.
  • 2). Chain Printer.

2). Non-Impact Printers :-

  • ➢ Non Impact Printers Print the Characters Without Using The Ribbon.
  • ➢ There Printers Print a Complete Page at a Time they are also Called as Page Printers.
  • ❖ Characteristics Of Non-impact Printers :-
  • ➢ Faster than Impact Printer
  • ➢ They are not Noisy.
  • ➢ High Quality
  • ➢ Support many Fonts & Different character Size.
  • ❖ There Printers are of Two Types :-
  • 1). Laser Printers
  • 2). Inkjet Printers

1). Laser Printers :-

  • There are Non impact page Printers.
  • ➢ They use laser Lights to Produce the dots needed to From the Characters to be Printed on a page.

2). Inkjet Printer :-

  • ➢ Inkjet Printers are Non Impact.character Printers based on a Relatively.
  • ➢ They Print Characters by Spraying small Drops of ink on to Paper.
  • ➢ Inkjet Printers Produce High Quality Output With Presentable Features.
